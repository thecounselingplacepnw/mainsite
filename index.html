"Welcome, I am glad you are here!"

Life often presents us with challenges that are difficult to navigate. Whether you are feeling anxious, depressed, overwhelmed, or facing a difficult situation, change is possible. By taking this first step towards counseling, you have already shown courage and hope.

At The Counseling Place, we will work together to:

  • Process challenging experiences in a confidential and supportive setting
  • Identify patterns in your thoughts, feelings, behaviors, and relationships
  • Gain insights into how those patterns are shaped by your experiences
  • Cultivate new skills and enhance your strengths for a thriving life

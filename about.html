I provide individual counseling in a welcoming, empathetic, and confidential setting. My practice is client-centered and strengths-based — you are the expert of your story, and your engagement is essential for progress.

Together we'll work to process experiences, identify patterns in thoughts and behaviors, gain insight, and cultivate new skills for a thriving life.

Outside the office, I love floating rivers on summer days, reading, game nights with family and friends, hiking in the Pacific Northwest, and exploring new places. Purple and teal are my signature colors — you'll see them everywhere here!

Therapeutic Approach

An integrated, evidence-based practice

Restoration Therapy

Examines how relational violations impact your sense of identity and safety — and helps restore both.

Interpersonal Neurobiology

Uses brain science to understand how relationships shape our development, and how new relationships can heal old wounds.

Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT)

Explores the connections between your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to create meaningful, lasting change.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Builds practical skills for emotion regulation and distress tolerance — tools you can use every single day.

Collaborative Problem Solving

Strengthens coping and communication skills — especially useful when navigating relationship and family dynamics.

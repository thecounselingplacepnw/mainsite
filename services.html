Counseling for life's challenges

Counseling is a collaborative partnership — you are the expert of your story, and Krissy is here to help you write the next chapter.

What We Offer

Services

Counseling is a collaborative partnership between you and your counselor. As you are the expert of your story, your engagement is essential for progress. Krissy's role is to support you as you explore challenges, offer clinical feedback, and introduce new coping strategies. Together, you can foster a strong partnership that facilitates positive change and helps you achieve your goals.

Anxiety, Depression, & Trauma

  • Understand what’s driving your stress, anxiety, or depression
  • Build grounding & coping skills for present-day distress
  • Work through difficult & traumatic experiences
  • Strengthen emotional regulation for everyday challenges
  • Move beyond survival mode with supportive routines

Relationships

  • Uncover unhealthy relationship patterns
  • Heal past & present relational wounds
  • Reduce conflict by strengthening communication & boundary-setting skills
  • Navigate complex relationship dynamics with clarity & confidence
  • Learn to regulate emotions & cope more effectively within relationships

Growth & Transitions

  • Explore & affirm your identity (gender, sexuality, ethnicity, spirituality)
  • Navigate life transitions with clarity & intention
  • Process grief & loss
  • Strengthen self-worth, confidence, & self-compassion
  • Align your life with your values

Cancellation Policy

24-hour notice required

Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice incur a $150 fee. Three missed appointments without notice may result in service termination. This time is reserved just for you — we appreciate your understanding.
